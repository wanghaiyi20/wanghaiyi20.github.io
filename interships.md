## China Electronics Spring Research Institute

**Intern**  
**Period:** 2025.09 - Present

I work on technical research related to **RISC-V matrix extensions**. My work includes investigating the design ideas of matrix and vector instruction extensions, analyzing how such extensions can be integrated with existing processor architectures, and studying representative application scenarios and market trends.

Specific tasks include:
- Surveying matrix / vector instruction extension design and their relationship with processor microarchitecture.
- Analyzing instruction mapping and execution flows for typical operators such as GEMM and convolution.
- Evaluating the impact of matrix extensions on computing performance, data movement, and hardware resource overhead.
- Summarizing design trade-offs for architecture research and implementation decisions.
